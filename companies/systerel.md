# Systerel

## Overview
Systerel is a French software engineering company specializing in formal methods, critical systems, and safety-critical software development. The company is known for expertise in the B Method and formal verification techniques.

## Activities
- Formal methods research and application (B Method, formal verification)
- Critical and safety-critical software development
- Railway and transportation systems (SNCF, rail industry)
- Nuclear and energy sector software
- Aerospace and defense systems
- Software verification, validation, and testing

## Technologies & Methods
- B Method and Event-B
- Model checking and formal verification
- Safety standards: EN 50128 (railway), DO 178 (aerospace), IEC 61508 (functional safety)
- V-cycle development methodology
- Code review and quality assurance

## Reputation
Systerel is highly regarded in the formal methods and safety-critical software community. The company is a leader in applying formal methods to real-world critical systems, particularly in France and Europe. Known for rigorous engineering practices and strong emphasis on verification.

## Location
Headquarters: Aix-en-Provence; offices in Paris and other locations.
