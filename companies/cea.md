# CEA (Commissariat à l'Énergie Atomique et aux énergies alternatives)

## Overview
CEA is a major French research and technology organization focused on nuclear energy, renewable energy, defense, and information technologies. It operates multiple research centers across France.

## Activities
- Nuclear energy research and development
- Renewable energy systems
- Materials science and nanotechnology
- Information technology and computing
- Defense and security research
- Life sciences and environmental research

## Technologies & Methods
- Nuclear reactor design and safety analysis
- High-performance computing
- Software development for scientific and industrial applications
- DevOps and cloud infrastructure
- Agile development methodologies

## Reputation
CEA is a prestigious French research institution with strong influence in nuclear energy and scientific computing. Known for rigorous standards and significant R&D investments. Offers both research and technical positions.

## Location
Multiple centers across France: Grenoble (primary HQ), Cadarache, Saclay, and others.

## Relevance to Candidate

Large research institution (~4,000+ employees across all divisions).
