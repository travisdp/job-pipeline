# Titagone (OCamlPro)

## Overview

Titagone is a Paris-based research and development consulting firm specializing in formal methods, programming languages, and software modernization. It operates as the commercial services division of what was originally founded as OCamlPro in 2011. The company is an INRIA spinoff founded by Fabrice le Fessant, a former 10-year INRIA researcher with deep expertise in formal verification and compiler design.

## Company Structure

- **Headquarters:** 21 rue de Châtillon, 75014 Paris, France
- **Employee Count:** 40+ engineers, including 15+ PhDs
- **Type:** Technology consulting and R&D firm (INRIA spinoff, certified research organization)
- **Leadership:** Fabrice Le Fessant (Founder, President), Muriel Shan Sei Fan (COO), Pierre Chambart (CTO)

## Core Activities

Titagone combines high-level research expertise with industrial consulting:
- Formal methods and theorem proving (Alt-Ergo SMT solver)
- OCaml language consulting and optimization
- Rust development services
- COBOL modernization (SuperBOL Studio)
- Blockchain development (Tezos platform)
- Compiler optimization
- WebAssembly (Owi symbolic execution framework)
- Security and cryptography verification
